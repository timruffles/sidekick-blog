SICP Blitz London

Running 5 Mondays starting June 3rd 2013, at 19.00.

Mailing list

Location

Camden Town Unlimited in Symes Mews (pass by a bright pink Age UK shop on the high street) next to Northern Line stop Mornington Crescent, or 5 mins walk from Camden Town tube if you’re on Bank branch.

Buzz at the door for Camden Town Unlimited (middle button), and we’ll send our mobile #s in case you’re a bit late. We’ll have a projector, WiFi of dubious reliability, and beers/tea as preferred!

Format

Bring a laptop, & any copies of the book etc you have. We’ll be in touch via email to suggest the 3 exercises for the first week. You’re welcome to choose your own too - we’ll spend the time discussing the exercises that people found interesting. Afterwards: pub!

As said in the initial signup feel free to use any language you like. Phil & I (the organisers) will be using Clojure.

Get the book

SICP is available free online, and in most mobile/ereader formats, or as a nicely chunky dead tree edition.
